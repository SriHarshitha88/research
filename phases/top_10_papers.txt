26. **Equations / formulas / burden logic**

Population Attributable Fraction (PAF):

[
PAF = \frac{\sum P_i(RR_i -1)}{\sum P_i(RR_i -1)+1}
]

Where:

* (P_i) = population proportion in exposure category i
* (RR_i) = relative risk for exposure category i
  (Page 16)

Attributable burden:

[
AB = PAF \times B
]

Where:

* (AB) = attributable burden
* (B) = baseline disease burden
  (Page 17)

DALY calculation:

[
DALY = YLL + YLD
]

(Page 11)

Years of Life Lost:

[
YLL = N \times L
]

Where:

* (N) = number of deaths
* (L) = standard life expectancy at age of death
  (Page 11)

Years Lived with Disability:

[
YLD = I \times DW \times L
]

Where:

* (I) = incident cases
* (DW) = disability weight
* (L) = duration of disability
  (Page 11)
