--------------------------------------------------
TASK 1: ENVIRONMENTAL BURDEN OF DISEASE (EBOD)
--------------------------------------------------

Core Logic of the Study

1. Climate change increases risk of certain diseases
2. Not all diseases are affected → must identify relevant ones
3. For those diseases:
   - Calculate Relative Risk (RR) due to climate exposure
4. Use RR to estimate:
   - Additional cases
   - Additional mortality + morbidity
5. Convert into additional DALYs lost
6. Final Output:
   → “Extra disease burden due to climate change in India”

--------------------------------------------------
STEP-BY-STEP PLAN
--------------------------------------------------

STEP 1 — Literature Review (Immediate Task)

-Do literature review

STEP 2 — Identify Diseases (India Context)

Decide:
- Which diseases from Egypt apply to India?
- Which need to be removed?
- Which new diseases should be added?

STEP 3 — Calculate Relative Risk (RR)

- Based on Indian studies/data
- RR = additional risk due to climate exposure

STEP 4 — Estimate Impact

Convert RR into:
- Additional cases
- Additional deaths
- Additional affected population

STEP 5 — Compute DALYs

Convert above into:
- YLL + YLD → DALYs

Final Output:
→ Additional DALYs due to climate change
